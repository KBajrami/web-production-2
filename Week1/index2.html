About Me

An animator and designer from New York, currently a student at Hunter college, majoring in Emerging Media. My passion has always been about animation, be it 2D or 3D, as well as games ranging from boardgames and videogames. Born in Albania, but raised in Newyork for over 20 years. I grew up playing all types of games, be it boardgames or Videogames and to this day I still continue to play them. Growing up I watched everything, from traditional cartoons to anime from Japan and so animation has been a part of my life for as I can remember.
